# Exploring AI in Education

I am currently a graduate student in Information Systems at Johns Hopkins University, with a background in computer science and economics from UIUC. I enjoy working with data and technology to solve real-world problems, especially in areas where analytical insights can improve decision-making and efficiency.

One area I care about is education, particularly how data and technology can improve the way students learn complex topics like programming and data analysis. From my own experience, many students struggle not because the material is too difficult, but because explanations are not clear or tailored to their level. With limited time, instructors cannot always provide personalized support to every student.

- Generative AI can help create customized study materials, such as summaries and practice questions, tailored to different learning levels.
- Generative AI can provide step-by-step explanations for technical topics (e.g., SQL or machine learning), which is especially helpful for students learning independently.
- Generative AI can assist instructors by generating drafts of assignments, feedback, or examples, allowing them to focus more on teaching and interaction.
